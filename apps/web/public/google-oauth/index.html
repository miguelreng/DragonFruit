DragonFruit connects your calendar to your work.

DragonFruit is a workspace for projects, tasks, docs, meeting notes, stickies, and lightweight agents. The Google integration helps users bring upcoming meetings into DragonFruit and turn meeting context into organized work.

How Google is used

Users may sign in with Google and may optionally connect Google Calendar. Calendar access is used to show upcoming meetings, support meeting reminders, and sync calendar-related work into DragonFruit.
