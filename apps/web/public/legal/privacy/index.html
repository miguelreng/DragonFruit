Privacy Policy

This policy explains how DragonFruit collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data, including data accessed through Google Sign-In and Google Calendar.

Overview

DragonFruit is a productivity workspace for projects, tasks, docs, meeting notes, stickies, and agents.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email, profile image, membership, and authentication data.
  • Workspace content such as projects, tasks, docs, comments, settings, and agent activity.
  • Technical information such as device, browser, IP address, and diagnostic logs.

Google user data

If you sign in with Google, DragonFruit may access your Google account identifier, email, name, and profile image to authenticate your DragonFruit account.

If you connect Google Calendar, DragonFruit may access event metadata including titles, descriptions, start and end times, location, attendees, and conferencing links to show meetings and organize related work.

Limited Use

DragonFruit's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements. DragonFruit does not sell Google user data and does not use it for advertising or general model training.

Retention and deletion

Users may disconnect Google Calendar in app settings and may request account or data deletion by contacting us.
